## What's Included in Social Media Management

- Content planning (shared via Airtable, Monday to Monday)
- Post creation: graphics, captions, scheduling
- Platform management: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn
- Organic engagement (liking, commenting, light interaction)
- DM management (Super Hero package only)
- Paid advertising campaign management
- Bi-monthly performance reporting (reach, engagement, clicks, growth)
- Strategy aligned with Meta for Business best practices
- Brand kit creation during onboarding (if needed)

## Process

1. **Discovery** — Learn about your business, goals, and target audience
2. **Strategy** — Design content calendars and campaigns to engage your audience
3. **Execution** — Create posts, graphics, videos; manage platforms; run campaigns
4. **Reporting** — Monitor performance, provide reports, refine strategy for continuous growth
